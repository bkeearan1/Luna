Lunaphiles St. Louis

Lunaphiles is a group located in St. Louis, missouri. We are cult like in spirit but without the poison punch line. We gather at the St. Louis County Library around 8:00pm on mondays, to use there telescopes to explore the craters of the giant rock sphere orbiting just outside our atmosphere. We also have discussions and light lectures on constilations and horoscopes. Current Projects we have a petition going around to change the name Monday to Moonday, and in the spring of March 20th, 2017 we will have an arts festival about the Vernal Equinox.
